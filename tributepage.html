Bangtan Sonyeondan

Korean Boy Band

  • December 17, 2012- The Official Twitter Account of BTS was launched.
  • June 09, 2013- The Official Member Line-up for BTS was Introduced to the Public.
  • June 11, 2013- Days after their very first Public appearance, Their very first MV "No More Dream" was released.
  • June 12, 2013- Day after their first MV was released, Their first Single Album "2 Cool 4 Skool" was released.
  • June 13, 2013- BTS had their first Stage Debut performing "No More Dream" at M Countdown and was considered their Debut Date.
  • July 09, 2013- BTS announced their Fandom's Official Name which is A.R.M.Y. or Adorable Representative M.C for Youth and is the Birth of ARMYs!
  • July 16, 2013- Their Second MV "We Are Bulletproof Pt. 2" was released.
  • September 02, 2013- Their Very First TV Show "Rookie King" was aired on SBS.
